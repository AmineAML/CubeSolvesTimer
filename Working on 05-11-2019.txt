Cube Solves Timer app
-Start button and Stop button are controlled by the space key from teh keyboard
-If the user want to start solving the cube, then s/he click on start
-If the user solve the cube, then s/he click on stop
-In the meantime the label on top-center displays an accurate timer with miliseconds
-No. of solves in this session(when the app opened to the app closed)
-Last 12 solves in this session(when the app opened to the app closed) seconds
-Other minimalistic informations
